About Our Research

Student engagement is essential for effective learning, yet falling asleep during lectures remains a common challenge. Our machine learning approach treats sleepiness as a form of "churn" in classroom engagement.

76.2% Test Accuracy Random Forest Model
Key Prediction Factors

Sleep Duration

Hours slept the previous night

Class Timing

Start time and weekday impact

Interest Level

Self-rated engagement with subject

Screen Time

Device usage before class
